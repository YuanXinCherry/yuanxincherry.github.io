Research


My general research interests lie in object re-identification, image retrieval, and deep metric learning, with a particular emphasis on the following topics:

  • Theory:
    Analysis and understanding of the generalization ability of various loss functions.

  • Algorithm:
    Development of effective deep learning algorithms for object re-identification and image retrieval.

  • Application:
    Application of learning algorithms in object re-identification, image retrieval, and deep metric learning.

Feel free to drop me an email if you are interested in collaborating with me on the above research topics.
