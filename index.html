Brief Biography

I am currently a Lecturer at School of Computer Science and Technology, Wuhan University of Science and Technology, China. My main research interests lie in object re-identification and image retrieval. I received my Ph.D. degree from School of Computer Science and Technology, Wuhan University of Science and Technology, China, in 2023, supervised by Prof. Xin Xu, Prof. Kai Zhang, and Prof. Zheng Wang. I am also a member in Visual Artificial Intelligence Lab. Before my Ph.D. study, I received my B.E. degree from School of Computer Science and Technology, Wuhan University of Science and Technology, China, in 2018.
